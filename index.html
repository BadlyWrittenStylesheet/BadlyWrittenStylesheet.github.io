Who am I?

As a teenager, my journey with computers began in the magical realm of block coding. Picture this: a young me, guided by my dad, exploring the digital universe through simple yet captivating games - maneuvering a character across a virtual board, collecting sparkling crystals, and immersing myself in the enchanting world of code. Fast forward a few vibrant years, and my expertise has evolved beyond blocks.

Now, I dance with Python, choreograph with HTML, paint with CSS, and orchestrate with Javascript. The transition from playful blocks to the sophisticated symphony of programming languages has been nothing short of exhilarating. Every day is a new chapter, a new opportunity to craft digital masterpieces. What once seemed like an unexpected detour in my life has transformed into a thrilling adventure. The journey continues, and I eagerly embrace the challenges, unraveling the secrets of web development, and savoring the joy of learning something new each day. Life with computers is not just a passion; it's a captivating odyssey that I never saw coming, but oh, how glad I am that it did.
